  1. Record Your Voice: Use a quiet environment and a device with a microphone to record a short voice sample. Speak naturally and clearly

  2. Save Your Recording: Save the audio file in a compatible format (e.g., MP3, WAV) on your device.

  3. Upload Your File: Click the "Select file" button to select the audio file from your device.

  4. Submit: Once you've selected the file, click the "Upload" button to submit your voice sample for analysis.

Your results will show up here once we've analysed your voice sample
